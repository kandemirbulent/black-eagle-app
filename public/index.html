How It Works

A cleaner path from event request to confirmed team

1

Apply and verify

Clients and staff submit registration details. Admin review keeps the system credible before access is granted.

2

Create the event

Clients submit staffing needs and complete the deposit so the request becomes a real live booking.

3

Collect the right applications

Suitable staff apply, the application window closes, and the strongest available team can be selected.

4

Confirm, deliver, and review

Clients see confirmed staff on the event, service is delivered, and feedback closes the quality loop.

Platform Access

One brand, two user journeys, one controlled operation

For clients

Create events, track payments, review confirmed staff, and leave feedback after the work is complete. The client experience is designed to reduce uncertainty before the event day.

+Approved-only access after review
+Deposit and final payment visibility
+Confirmed staff shown inside the event record

For staff

Staff build verified accounts, complete payment details, apply to suitable jobs, and track assignments without being flooded by irrelevant listings.

+Email verification and admin approval workflow
+Role and location-aware event visibility
+Assignment and earnings visibility after selection
Standards
