2. Booking & Deposits

All event bookings require a minimum deposit payment of one-third (33%) of the total quoted amount in order to secure the booking. Deposits are non-refundable once staff have been reserved for your event.

The remaining balance must be paid no later than 48 hours before the event date, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation is available up to 72 hours (3 days) before the event date. In this case, the full deposit will be refunded.
  • If cancellation is made within 72 hours (less than 3 days) before the event date, the 33% deposit payment becomes non-refundable.
  • This amount is treated as a cancellation fee to cover time and resources spent on staff arrangements, scheduling, and administrative preparation.
  • If cancellation is made after the event start time or on the event day itself, no refund will be issued.

4. Late Bookings (Last-Minute Orders)

For bookings made within 24 hours of the event start time, an additional 20% surcharge will be applied to the total amount due. This covers the extra administrative and logistical costs of arranging staff at short notice.
